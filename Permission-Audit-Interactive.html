The Permission Audit

A 10-minute mirror that shows you where you have been waiting for a permission only you can give.

Before you begin

There is a version of you that has been running the whole thing. The business. The household. The standard everyone has come to rely on. That version of you is capable and trusted, and has not stopped to ask one question in a very long time.

Who told you that you needed permission first.

This is not a test. There is no pass. This is a mirror. Answer for how you actually live, not how you intend to. The first answer is the true one.

The 90 second permission

Put one hand on your chest. Both feet on the floor. One slow breath. Now name the one thing you have been waiting for permission to do, or to stop doing. The real one.

I give myself
permission to ______.

Notice the part of you that flinched. That flinch is the gap. That is the exact place you have been living without permission. Say it again, an inch deeper. Then do the smallest possible version of it today.

The doorway

Most people will read this, feel the recognition, and let the old pattern close back over it by morning. You do not have to be most people.

Permission is the first doorway. Eight days where giving yourself permission stops being a moment you found once and becomes the baseline you operate from. The room where you are held while the new floor sets.
